The course expects you to reverse engineer binaries, often without source code, find vulnerabilities in them, and exploit them. Coming in cold, it is tough. Anyone learning exploitation will encounter the buffer overflow early. I was no different. To find exactly where in your input the return address sits, you reach for pwntools’ cyclic() and cyclic_find().
